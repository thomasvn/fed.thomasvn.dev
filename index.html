The Federal Reserve Architecture Diagram

I'm a visual learner, and recently I've taken an interest in the mechanics of the Fed.

I just finished reading The Lords of Easy Money by Christopher Leonard . It traces the Fed's response to the 2008 crisis, the easy-money decade that followed, and then Covid, and doesn't shy away from the messy consequences.

Throughout the book I kept filing away facts about the Fed and trying to build a mental model.

On the right is the architecture diagram I've come up with to best walk you through the Fed. All feedback welcome!

Why A Diagram

Some systems are easier to understand spatially than verbally. The Fed is one of them.
